Family Friendly Destinations

Why Choose Family Friendly Destinations?

Traveling with family can be a rewarding experience, but it requires careful planning to ensure that everyone has a great time. Family-friendly destinations offer activities and amenities that cater to all ages, making them ideal for creating lasting memories.

Top Five Family Friendly Destinations

No. 1: Orlando, Florida, USA

Known as the theme park capital of the world, Orlando is perfect for families who want magic, thrills, and endless entertainment.

Attractions

  • Walt Disney World Resort
  • Universal Orlando Resort
  • SeaWorld Orlando

Activities

  • Meet and greet with Disney characters
  • Ride rollercoasters at Universal Studios
  • Explore aquariums and dolphin shows at SeaWorld

Accommodation

  • Disney’s Art of Animation Resort
  • Universal’s Cabana Bay Beach Resort
  • Floridays Resort Orlando

No. 2: Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo blends futuristic fun with cultural charm, offering families a safe and exciting adventure.

Attractions

  • Tokyo Disneyland and DisneySea
  • Ueno Zoo and Ueno Park
  • TeamLab Planets (immersive digital art)

Activities

  • Try hands-on science at Miraikan Museum
  • Enjoy Japanese street food at Tsukiji Outer Market
  • Dress up in kimono for a family photo shoot

Accommodation

  • Hotel Gracery Shinjuku (Godzilla theme)
  • Hilton Tokyo Bay (near Disneyland)
  • Mimaru Tokyo (apartment-style for families)

No. 3: Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona offers sunny beaches, playful architecture, and a relaxed Mediterranean vibe that kids and adults enjoy together.

Attractions

  • Park Güell
  • Barcelona Aquarium
  • La Sagrada Família

Activities

  • Relax on Barceloneta Beach
  • Take a cable car ride up Montjuïc
  • Stroll Las Ramblas for street performers and markets

Accommodation

  • Novotel Barcelona City
  • Aparthotel Arai
  • Hotel Barcelona Princess

No. 4: Gold Coast, Australia

With golden beaches and world-class theme parks, the Gold Coast is a family paradise.

Attractions

  • Warner Bros. Movie World
  • Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Sea World Gold Coast

Activities

  • Surf lessons for beginners
  • Explore rainforests at Springbrook National Park
  • Ride the SkyPoint Observation Deck lift

Accommodation

  • Paradise Resort Gold Coast
  • Sea World Resort
  • Oaks Gold Coast Hotel

No. 5: London, United Kingdom

London mixes history, culture, and fun, making it a city that entertains the whole family

Attractions

  • Natural History Museum
  • London Zoo
  • Tower of London

Activities

  • Ride the London Eye
  • Take a Harry Potter Studio Tour
  • Picnic in Hyde Park

Accommodation

  • Premier Inn London County Hall
  • Staybridge Suites London Vauxhall
  • Novotel London Waterloo
